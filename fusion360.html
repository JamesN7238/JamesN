Demo Turbofan Engine

This project is a conceptual CAD model of a turbofan jet engine designed in Fusion 360. It highlights key components such as the fan, compressor, and turbine stages with detailed geometry intended to represent a realistic airflow path and housing design. This was my first major attempt at assembling multiple mechanical systems into a unified model, focusing on structural layout and internal clearance.
